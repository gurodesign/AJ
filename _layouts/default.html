Amando Ibañez
April 24, 1989 - August 15, 2023
Father   •   Husband   •   Brother   •   Friend

born Amando Pilapil Ibañez to Leticia Pilapil and Amando Ibañez, passed on August 15, 2023 of complications due to liver cancer. He is survived by his wife Krizzia Ibañez; his daughter, Celine Ibañez; his sisters, Aja Ibañez and Aljhecia Alolor; and his brothers, Eugene and Carmelo Ibañez. AJ was dedicated father and husband. As a talented musician he spent his days playing guitar, finger drumming beats on his MPC and producing music for himself and others. AJ recently completed his Cybersecurity certification at City College, and as an aspiring technologist had ambitions to explore the field of data science.
